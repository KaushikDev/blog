Blog#1: CES 2018

10th January 2018

Hi. I am sure you are excited about the CES 2018. I am!!.
Or, if you have been living under a rock, CES is Consumer Electronics Show held every year. And this year is being organised in Las Vegas from 09thJan-12thJan.
The show features almost every player from tech world to display and showcase their new innovations and products.
The stars of the Show this year, so far have been Samsungs's 146 inch micro OLED Wall TV , NVIDIA's 65inch BFGD(Big Format Gaming Display) with 120Hz sync, a treat to eyes for gaming enthusiasts and gamers. Razer also sported their new stealth like ultrabook powered by a phone. Yes, you read that right!! A phone powering an ultrabook. Not to leave out ACER who managed to introduce the 8 mm thick(or thin?) ultrabook, world's first. And VIVO beating giants like Apple and Samsung in introducing first finger print unlock feature embedded in display. !!
There's a lot more going on. I will be sharing more as we go forward. Share with me what excited you most or what would you like to see next year.
